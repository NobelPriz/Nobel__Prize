Annie Ernaux The Nobel Prize in Literature 2022 Born: 1 September 1940, Lillebonne, France Residence at the time of the award: France Prize motivation: “for the courage and clinical acuity with which she uncovers the roots, estrangements and collective restraints of personal memory” Language: French
