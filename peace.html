Ales Bialiatski

Ales Bialiatski The Nobel Peace Prize 2022 Born: 25 September 1962, Vyartsilya, Karelia, Russia Residence at the time of the award: Belarus Prize motivation: “The Peace Prize laureates represent civil society in their home countries. They have for many years promoted the right to criticise power and protect the fundamental rights of citizens. They have made an outstanding effort to document war crimes, human right abuses and the abuse of power. Together they demonstrate the significance of civil society for peace and democracy”
